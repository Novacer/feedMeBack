About Us

We are a group of entrepreneurial individuals who wants to make a change in the society by encouraging customers to give constructive feedback. In this way, customers feel that their voice are being heard and companies are given the resources to understand their potential customers' needs and demands.

Feel free to contact us at wmloh@uwaterloo.ca for more details!


Our Philosophy


Businesses


In today's data-driven economy, knowing how your consumers' behaviours and demands are imperative to keep the business growing.


By using a platform where users congregate to evaluate companies based on their service and products, businesses could stand out amongst the crowd if customers' voices are heard.



Our services enable a more contemporary approach to customer relationship management (CRM) which could capture potential customers' attention.


Customers


Who does not want a free cookie? With just a scan using your smartphone camera and filling out a short form, the cookie will be yours!


If you are happy with the services or products of a certain company, this is the place to give your note of encouragement. If you are not, then this is the place to share what you think could have been improved.


When people are able to freely about to convey feedback, companies will certainly know that their reputation is at stake. This means that businesses will want to maintain business integrity to ensure that every one of you is satisfied.
